Que4. Naming convention 
ans.    1. follow lower camel case.
        2. Variable names cannot start with numbers or special characters.
        3. There should not be any spaces in variable names.
        4. Variable names are case-sensitive.
        5. You cannot use reserved keywords as variable names.
